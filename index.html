How To Play

This is a flag memory game by matching cards. There are two difficulty levels, easy and hard. To play simply click a pair of cards to flip them over. If they match they will stay face up, if they unmatch they will flip back over. The aim of the game to to find all matching pairs of flags. You can restart the game at anytime.Enjoy!
